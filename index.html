About the Project

MindMaze is a teen-friendly mental wellness platform designed to help young people navigate stress, anxiety, emotional confusion, and daily challenges. What started as an idea has grown into a full-fledged product built with one goal — to provide a safe, supportive, and interactive space for the youth of today.

The Problem We Address

Increasing stress among teens
Lack of safe emotional spaces
Dependence on unhealthy coping habits
Fear of judgement and misunderstanding

Our Key Features

Anonymous Chat Groups
Share feelings safely without revealing identity.
Stress-Buster Games
Relax the mind through engaging mini-games.
Emotional Journey Tracker
Visualize your mood and mental growth.
Breathing Exercises
Quick calming routines for anxiety.
Daily Mood Check-ins
Track emotions and reflect every day.
Emergency Helplines
Immediate support when needed.

Future Vision

We aim to make MindMaze a global, free, non-profit platform accessible to all teenagers. Our dream is to build a world where every teen — no matter their background — has someone to rely on mentally, emotionally, and safely.
